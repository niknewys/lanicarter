Maternity Photography Sunshine Coast

Maternity photography is all about celebrating women. Pregnancy is not just the creation of life, but it is connecting a woman to another soul. There are quite literally two hearts in one vessel, how crazy is that to think about. There is so much power and beauty in the changes a woman’s body experiences during this time and that is worth documenting.

My maternity photography sessions take around 40 minutes and are shot on location around the Sunshine Coast. I recommend starting your session in something that hugs your curves. A fitted dress, or a flowy dress that is fitted in all the right places. We want to show off those curves, so keep that in mind when selecting your outfit. I will also really encourage (pressure/force) you to take it all off and pose in just your underwear. Just you, your belly and the sunset. Natural maternity photos are my specialty.

My maternity shoots are designed to be one on one with the mother to be.

If there is a partner or other children you wish to be involved we can take some photos with them first, but then have them leave us to concentrate on the star of the show. This time is about you and your belly!

“No one else will ever know the strength of my love for you. After all, you’re the only one who knows the sound of my heartbeat from the inside.”
